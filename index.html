What we believe
about this process

The college application is not a bureaucratic obstacle. It is one of the first real opportunities a young person has to articulate who they are, what they value, and where they want to go. Done well, it is clarifying. Done without guidance, it is needlessly hard.

"Don't measure yourself by what you have accomplished, but by what you should have accomplished with your ability." John Wooden

Strategy without anxiety

We help families understand what top schools are actually looking for and build a plan around each student's genuine strengths.

Find your voice

With deep expertise in narrative craft, we help students find their voice rather than polish it into something unrecognizable.

No pretense, just results

We are direct, honest, and thorough. We tell students what actually works, not what they want to hear.

✦   About

About Astra Advisors

Astra Advisors was founded on a simple belief: every student deserves the kind of guidance once reserved for a lucky few.

We are a boutique practice led by Lillianne Montague, an educator, scholar, and EdTech professional whose career has been shaped by a single question: how do we help young people reach their full potential?

We offer a combination of academic and practical expertise to every student we work with. We understand what competitive schools look for in a personal statement. We have studied how educational institutions work from the inside and use that knowledge to give students a genuine edge. The modern college process is demanding. With the right guidance, it is entirely manageable.

✦   Services

How we work
with students

Focused, hourly sessions tailored to where a student is in the process, whether building a school list sophomore year or refining a personal statement the week before a deadline. Every session is one-on-one with one of our advisors. No handoffs, no templated feedback.

Essay Coaching

Finding Your Voice

From the personal statement to school-specific supplements, we help students write essays that are specific, honest, and memorable. We don't rewrite. We help students write better. The voice in the final essay is entirely their own.

School Selection

Building the Right List

A well-built school list is one of the highest-leverage decisions in the process. We help students think clearly about fit, selectivity, and strategy to produce a list that is both ambitious and realistic.

Application Strategy

Presenting the Full Picture

Activities lists, recommendation letter strategy, demonstrated interest, Early Decision versus Early Action: the application has many moving parts. We help students see it as a coherent whole and present themselves accordingly.

Interview Prep

Showing Up With Confidence

Selective schools pay close attention to interviews. We run realistic practice sessions and help students develop the clarity and composure to represent themselves well in any room.

Academic Tutoring

Strengthening the Foundation

A strong application starts in the classroom. We offer subject-level tutoring across core academic disciplines and SAT and ACT tutoring for students looking to deepen their understanding, improve grades, or prepare for standardized tests.

Honors Program Advising

Beyond the Ivies

Advanced direct-to-graduate programs offer exceptional students a world-class education with unique advantages. We help students identify, apply to, and make the strongest possible case for competitive honors programs that are often overlooked and frequently transformative.

"The doors of wisdom are never shut." Benjamin Franklin

We work with high school students at every stage, from freshmen mapping out a four-year plan to seniors in the final push. We also work with international students navigating the U.S. process, and first-generation applicants for whom the system is genuinely unfamiliar territory.
